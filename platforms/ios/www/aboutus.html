About Latorre Insurance

     For over a decade, Latorre Insurance has been providing custom insurance policies to the Charlotte, Monroe, and Greensboro areas. We take the time to sit down with you to determine your insurance needs and how to best achieve these goals. Oftentimes, companies concentrate on selling products as quickly as possible, and they view taking the time to understand their clients as wasted time and money. This is not the philosophy at Latorre Insurance. Richard Latorre and his skilled team focus on solving your insurance problems. An insurance agent at Latorre Insurance will never rush you out the door with a policy that isn't right for you. We believe that it is important to know and understand each and every one of our clients, giving us the ability to present them with a variety of options based on their individual needs and budgets.

     The main goal of our team is to provide only the best in insurance, offering you outstanding coverage and affordable premiums at low and affordable prices. At Latorre Insurance, you are more than another policy holder; you are our friend and neighbor, which is why we treat all of our clients with the respect and understanding that they deserve, while also providing them with the best insurance policies available.

     With offices in Charlotte, Monroe, and Greensboro, North Carolina, our agents are ready to answer your questions in both English and Spanish, offering you solutions that best achieve your goals as an individual client. Because we are independent agents, working with top carriers, like The Hartford, Progressive, Builders Manual, Erie, National General, and Blue Cross Blue Shield of North Carolina, we are able to match a policy to any unique situation. We will never take a pre-built policy and attempt to force it to fit your needs.

Latorre Insurance Group has convenient Charlotte, Greensboro, and Monroe offices. You can compare insurance rates from the largest names in insurance with experts from any one of our North Carolina insurance locations.
